Details

Presence is not a performance skill learned by a degree — it's about energy and feel. It is the practice of fully being where you are: with the people you are with, in the space you are in, in your body.

These workshops are small by design. We will slow down, pay attention, and practice the fundamentals: breathing, grounding, looking, listening, and speaking from somewhere real.

No experience needed. Whether you are preparing for a big talk, a job interview, a difficult conversation, or just curious — the work starts in the same place: your body.
